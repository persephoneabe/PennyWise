WHY PENNY WISE

Penny Wise is a financial literacy application with an extensive library of learning materials alongside practical implementations in order to create fiscally responsible consumers

Penny Wise was developed to combat the verbose and complex descriptions of efficient use of financial instruments. By directly accessing the user's bank account balances, and keeping track of the user's weekly income and expenditures, as well as estimating the amount of money put aside for various savings accounts, it can project what their future disposable incomes are for the upcoming month. It is always hard for people to keep track and manage their finances in a timely and intelligent fashion. This app makes it convenient for bank users to estimate how much extra money they can spend without overspending.
